Get started

To get started, enter the "topic" you want to stream tweets for (i.e. Trump, COVID-19), the length of time (in minutes) to you want to stream for, and your email. Once your stream has completed, you will receive a link to a unique URL where you can access your data and create visualizations.

Note: The rate of streaming fluctuates depending on the popularity of the topic, but for popular topics it is approximately 5000 tweets/min. For security reasons, you can only enter one email at a time
